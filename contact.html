Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our facilities management services.

MOOZEN provides comprehensive facilities management services including Operation & Maintenance, Hospitality Services, Cleaning Services, Staff Management, and Transportation & Logistics. We serve various sectors including government, healthcare, education, and corporate facilities.

We provide 24/7 emergency support with response times of 2-4 hours for urgent issues. For routine maintenance and service requests, we typically respond within 24 hours and schedule services based on priority and client requirements.

Yes, while our main offices are in Riyadh, Jeddah, and Dammam, we provide services throughout Saudi Arabia. We have mobile teams and partnerships that allow us to serve clients in smaller cities and remote locations.

MOOZEN is ISO 9001:2015 certified for Quality Management, ISO 14001:2015 for Environmental Management, and ISO 45001:2018 for Occupational Health and Safety. We also comply with Saudi Arabian standards and regulations for all our services.

We implement rigorous quality control measures including regular inspections, client feedback systems, performance metrics tracking, and continuous staff training. Our quality assurance team conducts monthly audits and maintains detailed service reports for all clients.
