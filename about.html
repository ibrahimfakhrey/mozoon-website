About MOOZEN

Excellence in facilities management since 2009

Our Story

Founded in 2009, MOOZEN has grown from a small facilities management startup to become one of Saudi Arabia's most trusted and comprehensive service providers.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to provide high-quality maintenance and operation services that exceed client expectations. Over the years, we have expanded our capabilities to include hospitality services, cleaning solutions, staff management, and transportation logistics.

Today, MOOZEN serves over 200 clients across various sectors including government institutions, commercial enterprises, industrial facilities, healthcare centers, and educational institutions. Our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement has made us a preferred partner for organizations seeking reliable facilities management solutions.
